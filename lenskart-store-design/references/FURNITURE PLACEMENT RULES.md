# Furniture Placement Rules — derived from the Design Manual (v1.0)

Source: `DESIGN MANUAL.pdf` (145 pp). This distills every fixture/furniture placement,
sequencing, clearance and count rule into one reference for generating store layouts.
Rules are grouped so they can be encoded directly into the layout engine. All dimensions
in mm unless noted. "BOB" = bottom of beam/bulkhead; "FFL" = finished floor level.

---

## 1. Site / shell prerequisites (Recce validation)

These gate whether a layout is even viable.

- Usable area **min 500 sqft** on a single floor plate (excluding toilet, staircase cutouts). Exceptions need panel approval.
- Store **width min 3650, ideal 3900**.
- Entry height **min 2400 from FFL** (exception to 2100 per NBC, not below).
- Internal height **min 2700 from FFL**; **BOB min 2500**.
- Entry door **min 1050 W × 2400 H**.
- Rolling shutter: clearance below RS drum **≥ 2400 from FFL**.
- Existing glazing retained if in good condition.
- **1 washroom mandatory** within the store (exhaust mandatory).

---

## 2. Zoning model (front → back gradient)

The store reads as a depth gradient from entrance to rear, matching the customer-experience flow:

| Sequence | Experience | Zone | Location |
|---|---|---|---|
| Attract | Facade & signage | (facade) | Shopfront / glazing |
| Establish | Greetr (host welcome) | RETAIL | At/just inside entry |
| Inspire | Lifestyle displays | RETAIL | Perimeter walls + front |
| Inform | Eye test & consult | CLINIC | Toward back |
| Engage | LENSBAR / 3D tryon / repair-pickup | DISCUSSION & BILLING | Central → rear |
| Accomplish | Checkout | DISCUSSION & BILLING | Central back wall |

Spatial arrangement (per zoning diagrams):

- **RETAIL** — wall fixtures line both side walls; runs from the shopfront back along the perimeter. Floor fixtures sit in the central retail field.
- **DISCUSSION & BILLING** — discussion tables in the centre flanking the Euro Center; POS/cash centrally on the back wall.
- **CLINIC** — clustered at the **back end** of the store.
- **BACK OF HOUSE / STORE** — rearmost corner, behind a door.
- **TOILET** — rearmost, ideally off a common passage (not through retail or behind cash).

## 3. Wall fixtures (RETAIL)

**Brand sequence — Premium side wall** (order as you enter):
JJ-EYE (must be visible on entry) → OD-EYE → JJ-SUN. VC-SUN preferably follows JJ-SUN or stays in close proximity (VC-SUN may instead go on the Euro Center floor fixture).

**Brand sequence — Value side wall** (order as you enter):
LK Air → VC-EYE → Hooper.

- **CLens** units are used as a **breaker between categories** along the wall run.
- Wall fixture height is 2400 (C Lens / JJ Super Hybrid / Looker / Sink unit all H2400).

**Mirrors (interleaved with wall fixtures):**

- Standard **300mm mirror unit after every wall fixture**; at minimum a mirror **between every 2 fixtures**.
- Columns on site may be used as on-site mirrors, **min 250mm width**.
- Fixture sides may be used as on-site mirrors where space is constrained.
- **Corner boxing mirror min 300mm**.

---

## 4. Floor fixtures (RETAIL)

- **Euro Center: min 1 per store** (for tent-pole / collection displays). VC-SUN can live here. **[XP/FB] Run 4 euros (two double-euro blocks, or a 2×2 island); euros are turned 90° and JOINED into solid blocks — never leave a lone half-euro padding next to a euro.**
- **Blue Zero: 1 per store** — placed **visible as you enter**. **[FB] Default placement: against the FRONT GLAZING on the right-hand side** (this is the client's stated preference; the expert plan instead parks it at the rear of a euro block — glazing-right wins unless told otherwise).
- **~~Discussion Tables: min 2 per store~~ — SUPERSEDED. [FB] DO NOT use discussion tables.** The client has removed them and the expert plan uses none; the euro blocks + billing pod replace their function. Do not place D-TABLE fixtures.
- **POS / Cash counter:**
  - Placed **centrally on the back wall, in front of the clinics**.
  - In narrower stores, a left- or right-aligned cash placement is acceptable.

---

## 5. Circulation & clearances (critical numbers)

- **Retail aisle:** maintain **1050–1200 mm minimum** between floor fixtures and wall fixtures (1050–1200 ideal in retail).
- **Passages (BOH):** **800–900 mm** clear ideal.
- **Behind POS counter:** ideal **750 mm**, **min 600 mm**.
- **Clinics:** **750 mm clear passage** to clinics; **opening size 750 mm** to clinic and to BOH/Store.

---

## 6. Clinics

**Count by proto (store revenue tier):**

| Proto | Total clinics | Operational | Non-op |
|---|---|---|---|
| Up to ₹12 L | 2 | 1 (RO clinic) | 1 |
| ₹12–15 L | 3 | 2 (1 Regular + 1 RO) | 1 |
| > ₹15 L | 4 | 3 (2 Regular + 1 RO) | 1 |

Rules:

- **All clinics positioned toward the back** of the store.
- **1 clinic must have storage + Sink Unit.**
- **1 clinic must have Remote Optometry (RO) provision.**
- If there is a mezzanine/first floor (MF/FF), ensure **at least 1 clinic on the ground level** (the RO clinic).

**Clinic types & sizes:**

- Standard / Regular: **1500 × 2400**, viewing range 1800 (Nidek TS310).
- Compact (MC5S, PC or joystick): **1350–1500 × 1800**, viewing range 1000.
- Remote Optometry (RO): **2500 × 1500** or **1500 × 2400**, viewing range 1800.
- Mingsing: 1500 × 1800, viewing range 300.

**Tight-store storage tactics:** add a no-sink cabinet in the 2nd clinic; add overhead storage above the clinic chair; provide cabinet/storage in passageways where possible.

## 7. Seating

- **Bench seating: at least 1 per store** — preferably near the POS counter / clinics. Alternate locations: near the Euro Center or at the facade.
- **> ₹15 L proto:** a lounge table / sofa seating may be added based on space.

## 8. Back of House / Store

- **All BOH to have a door.**
- Minimum furniture & equipment:
  - Dining / Repair / QC table: **1 min – 3 max**
  - Storage Rack: 1
  - Staff Rack: 1
  - Water Dispenser: 1
  - UPS Rack: 1

---

## 9. Toilet

- **1 mandatory**, with exhaust; finishes/fittings per LK standard.
- **Min sizing 1500 × 1500** or **1200 × 2250** (toilet finish sheets show min 1500 × 1200).
- Ideally accessible from a **common passage independent of BOH/Store**.
- **Avoid** toilet access from retail, and from behind cash, unless unavoidable.

---

## 10. Screens & signage placement

**TV screens:**

- **55" preferred minimum** (where BOB ≈ 3 m); **49"** where BOB is 2.8 m.
- Locations: at the **facade glazing**, and **1 above the Euro Center**. Mounted **back-to-back**.
- Bottom of TV **not less than 1950 on Euro Center**, **2100 for other TVs**.
- Must be able to display **QMS** (queue management); place for customer visibility.
- Low-height stores: locate TV at the cash back wall.

**Signage:**

- Stack signage mounted on fluted panel at **cash back**.
- **"Free Eye Test" signage at glazing.**

## 13. Merch-mix logic (fixture counts per brand)

The manual carries a per-brand merch-mix matrix (graphic table, p.10). Logic to apply:

- **MAX** = maximum fixture count for a brand.
- **TARGET** = ideal fixture count for a brand category.
- **CONTROL THRESHOLD** = the lower of MAX and (TARGET + 20%).

Brand categories: Premium (JJ-EYE, OD-EYE, JJ-SUN, LK-AIR), Value (VC-EYE, Hooper Kids),
Other (Blue Zero, Contact Lens), Super Premium (Branded/Fossil, LPL), Misc (Accessories).
The exact per-brand numbers should be read from the p.10 matrix when fixing counts.

## 14. Density, clinics-as-L, safety & finishes  [XP — from the expert field plan `BASE 2 FP.dxf`]

These additions were missing from v1.0 and are confirmed against a real expert-placed store. They override looser earlier numbers where they conflict.

**Circulation (REVISED — tighter than §5):**

- Shopping aisles are designed to the **functional minimum 1100–1200 mm**, not wider. Reclaim all "spare" aisle width as additional fixtures/euros. Wheelchair turning still 1500 only where required (entry, billing).
- Retail/trade area should occupy **~85–88% of the floor depth**; BOH + clinics are squeezed into the rear ~12–18% (not a 40% back band).

**Floor fixtures (REVISED — denser than §4):**

- **Euro Centers: run TWO double-euro blocks (4 euros total)** — one each side of the centreline — framing a **1200 mm central aisle**, with **~1150 mm aisles** to the side walls. (Min 1 is the absolute floor; 4 is the expert norm for this size of plate.)
- Wall fixtures start at the **glazing line** and run continuously to the back partition; brand-tag each fixture.

**Clinics (REVISED — see §6):**

- **Default 2-clinic arrangement = an L-shape wrapping one back corner**: one leg along the side wall (the SINK clinic), one leg along the back wall (the RO clinic), sharing the inside corner as a common corridor. The L may be mirrored to either back corner. **[OPT] When picking from the canonical combos (§15.8), try the L-shape `2B` FIRST — it is usually the most space-efficient and is what the expert field plans gravitate to; prefer it over the disjoint twin-room `2C` unless 2B doesn't fit. See optimization guide §16.1.**
- **Each clinic opens via a CURTAIN on its lower / corridor-facing side** (layer `LK-CURTAIN`), never on the back wall. Curtains replace hinged doors (no swing space).
- Keep the RO/remote clinic equipped: `PHOROPTER`, `MINGSING`, `MOROTISED TABLE-TRIAL SET`, `TRIAL SET ROC`, `MIC`, `32INCH VC FOR CLINIC`, `DOCTOR STOOL`. Sink clinic: `CLINIC - CABINET SINK`, `CLINIC - MOP CLOSET`, `CLINIC SCREEN`, `CHAIR UNIT`, `TRIAL SET CABINET`.

**Mandatory safety equipment (NEW — was missing):**

- **Fire extinguishers: ≥3 per store** (roughly 1 per 40–50 m²), distributed front / mid / back, mounted on columns or walls. Layer `F-FIRE EXTINGUISHER`. This is a hard requirement for sign-off.

**Screens (REVISED — see §10):**

- Ship **4× 55"**: **2 facade** at the glazing (street-facing / QMS) **+ 2 back-to-back on the euro centre**. A single screen is not acceptable.

**Billing / dispensing (REVISED):**

- Prefer a compact **Billing Counter-1350 at the end of the central aisle**; place the **AR station separately in the BOH** rather than welding billing+AR+lensometer into one pod.

**Finishes as zoning (NEW):**

- Tag floor/wall finishes by zone even in a planning DXF: **tiles in clinics + toilet**, **fluted panel on the cash-back wall**, **carpet/paint in retail + BOH**. Layers: `LK-TILES FINISH`, `LK-FLUTED FINISH`, `LK-PAINT FINISH`, `PLANO-CARPET`.

## 15. Counting & target conventions  [FB] (client direction, 2026-06-19 — apply by default, do not re-ask)

1. **"Fixture count" = wall + floor DISPLAY units only.** When the client states a fixture target (e.g. "19 fixtures"), it counts **wall display units** (JJ SH, CLENS, VC HD, LOOKER, etc.) **+ floor display fixtures** (Euro Centers, Blue Zero). It does **NOT** include billing/POS, greeter, pickup, AR, lensometer, benches, mirrors, clinic furniture, BOH equipment, or TV screens.

2. **Fixture targets are APPROXIMATE.** The number is a guide, not a hard constraint: going **a bit over is preferred** (more product on display); going **a bit under is acceptable** if the store genuinely lacks space. Aim at it; don't agonise over an exact hit.

3. **[FB] Existing-toilet DOOR swing + approach must stay clear.** When reusing an existing toilet, find its door opening/swing in the shell and keep both the swing arc and a real approach passage clear — never butt a clinic/BOH partition against the toilet door. Route the toilet access off the same shared service corridor that feeds the clinics (a common passage, not through retail, not behind cash). Learned from a BASE 3 miss where a clinic wall blocked the WC door.

4. **[FB] Clinic opening is on the LONG side AND faces a clear corridor — verify both.** The curtain/opening must sit on one of the clinic's two long (deeper, e.g. 2400) walls, and that wall must face a genuinely clear shared corridor (≥1100–1200 mm). Opening on the short (e.g. 1500) wall is a violation even if it faces the corridor. Always re-check this in the audit.

5. **[FB] NEVER overlap a column or beam — this is a hard constraint.** Before placing anything, extract **every** structural element: layers `column`, `column hatch`, **`beam`**, AND any **free-standing mid-floor columns** (BASE 3 has one at the centre of the hall that is easy to miss). Build the full obstruction list and audit every fixture (euros, billing, wall units, clinics, BOH, bench) against it. A fixture may **butt against** a column/beam face (align to it, touching) but its footprint must never overlap one. A previous BASE 3 draft put a euro island and the billing counter directly on the mid-hall column — do not repeat. Re-run the column/beam overlap audit before every sign-off.

6. **[FB] Seating must leave walk-around space around euros/islands.** Don't pinch a bench against the end of a Euro Center or anywhere it blocks circulation around an island — leave a real path (≥600–750 mm, ideally a full aisle) so customers can walk all the way around the euro. Put benches in open waiting space (near billing/clinics), never hard against a display island.

7. **[FB] Use ONLY the new trimmed library `BASE LIBRARY.dxf`.** The old `FURNITURE BLOCKS.dxf` (+ clinic block files) are **retired** — never source blocks from them. The trimmed library removed: `LOOKER`, `NESTING TABLES`, discussion tables, sofas, `POS`, generic `CABINET`, `PICK UP COUNTER`, and the `55"/49"/43"` TV blocks. (See optimization guide §15.1 for the full kept-block list and substitutions: TV → rectangle on `LK-TV SCREEN`; storage racks → labelled rectangles; pickup counter → `Pickup table PC` + rectangle.)

8. **[FB] Place clinics from the CANONICAL arrangements, do not invent positions.** Use the pre-made room blocks — **SINK room `A$C15b5f610` (2600×1700)** and **RO room `A$Cc304dd19` (2600×1700, ignore its stray dimension)** — placed in one of the fixed relative arrangements from `2 CLINIC LIBRARY.dxf` / `3 CLINIC LIBRARY.dxf` (cataloged in optimization guide §15.2–15.3: 2-clinic = twin-landscape / L-shape / twin-portrait+corridor; 3-clinic = triple-row / portrait-landscape-portrait / mixed-row). Only design a custom clinic position if **none** of the canonical arrangements physically fit the shell. Each room opens on a long side — orient the arrangement so openings face a clear corridor and clear the toilet door.
